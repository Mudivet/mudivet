About Mudi Vet

Two decades of steady, expert animal care.

Mudi Veterinary Clinic has served the community for 20 years, supporting both corporate and domestic animal care needs with practical clinical attention and reliable follow-through.

The clinic provides expert consultation services for pets, livestock, poultry, aquaculture, small ruminants, and herd health programmes, with guidance from professionals trained overseas and experienced in local animal health challenges.

Services

Complete clinic, farm, and home veterinary support.

Preventive Care

  • Vaccinations for Newcastle disease, FMD, anthrax, rabies, CBPP, and dog/cat core vaccines
  • Deworming and parasite control for fleas, ticks, and helminths
  • Routine wellness examinations for livestock, poultry, dogs, and cats
  • Nutritional counselling, feed planning, and mineral supplementation

Diagnostic Services

  • Laboratory testing including blood counts, faecal exams, urinalysis, milk testing, and disease screening
  • Imaging and ultrasound for pregnancy diagnosis, organ assessment, and musculoskeletal checks
  • Disease investigation, outbreak response, post-mortems, and zoonotic disease surveillance

Treatment & Surgery

  • General surgery including wound repair, castration, caesarean sections, and tumour removal
  • Medical treatment with IV fluids, antibiotic therapy, and pain management
  • Dental care including scaling, extraction, and oral health assessment
  • Spay and neuter services for dogs and cats

Reproductive & Herd Health

  • Artificial insemination for cattle and goats
  • Pregnancy monitoring, calving assistance, and neonatal care
  • Scheduled herd health programmes and farm visits

Poultry & Small Animals

  • Poultry health services including flock vaccination, biosecurity, and Newcastle disease control
  • Small ruminant care, PPR vaccination, foot rot support, and pneumonia management
  • Aquaculture support for tilapia and catfish pond farmers

Community & Extension Services

  • Farmer training workshops on animal husbandry and disease recognition
  • Mobile vet visits to remote villages and smallholder farms
  • Veterinary certification for animal movement, sale, and export
  • Zoonosis awareness education for rabies, brucellosis, and anthrax

Companion Animal Extras

  • Microchipping and rabies tag registration
  • Grooming including bathing, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and coat care
  • Pet boarding for short stays
