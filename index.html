About Us

Expert Care, Rooted in Community

For over two decades, Mudi Veterinary Clinic has been at the heart of animal health in Lilongwe and the surrounding region. We bring together internationally-trained veterinary specialists and deep local knowledge to deliver care that is both world-class and community-centred.

  • Founded over 20 years ago and continuously growing to serve more animals and farming communities across Malawi.
  • Expert veterinarians trained and certified overseas — bringing global best practices to local care.
  • Comprehensive corporate animal health services for farms, agribusinesses, and large-scale livestock operations.
  • Dedicated domestic/companion animal services — caring for your beloved dogs, cats, and small pets with equal attentiveness.
  • Expert consultation services covering diagnosis, herd health management, disease prevention, and nutrition planning.
  • Mobile vet units available for home visits within a 5 km radius — bringing the clinic to your doorstep.
What We Offer

Comprehensive Veterinary Services

From routine wellness check-ups to complex surgery and emergency care — we provide full-spectrum veterinary services for all animals.

💉

Preventive Care

  • Vaccinations — Newcastle, FMD, Anthrax, Rabies, CBPP, dog & cat core vaccines
  • Deworming & parasite control (fleas, ticks, helminths)
  • Routine wellness exams for livestock, poultry, dogs & cats
  • Nutritional counselling, feed planning & mineral supplementation
🔬

Diagnostic Services

  • Laboratory testing — blood counts, faecal exams, urinalysis, milk testing
  • Imaging & ultrasound — pregnancy diagnosis, organ & musculoskeletal assessment
  • Disease investigation, outbreak response & post-mortems
  • Zoonotic disease surveillance
🏥

Treatment & Surgery

  • General surgery — wound repair, castration, C-sections, tumour removal
  • Medical treatment — IV fluids, antibiotic therapy, pain management
  • Dental care — scaling, extraction, oral health assessment
  • Spay & neuter services for dogs and cats
🐄

Reproductive & Herd Health

  • Artificial insemination for cattle and goats
  • Pregnancy monitoring, calving assistance & neonatal care
  • Scheduled herd health programmes and farm visits
🐓

Poultry & Small Animals

  • Flock vaccination, biosecurity & Newcastle disease control
  • Small ruminant care — PPR vaccination, foot rot & pneumonia management
  • Aquaculture support for tilapia and catfish pond farmers
🌾

Community & Extension Services

  • Farmer training workshops on animal husbandry & disease recognition
  • Mobile vet visits to remote villages and smallholder farms
  • Veterinary certification for animal movement, sale & export
  • Zoonosis awareness education — Rabies, Brucellosis, Anthrax
🐾

Companion Animal Extras

  • Microchipping & rabies tag registration
  • Grooming — bathing, nail trimming, ear cleaning, coat care
  • Pet boarding for short stays
